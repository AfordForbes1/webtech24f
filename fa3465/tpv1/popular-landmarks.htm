Top Landmarks to Explore in Belize

Belize is home to some of the world's most stunning natural and historical landmarks. Discover the Belize Barrier Reef, the Great Blue Hole, and ancient Mayan ruins such as Altun Ha and Caracol. Experience the breathtaking beauty of these iconic locations, which reflect the rich culture and history of Belize.

Altun Ha

Altun Ha is one of Belize's most famous Mayan ruins. Located north of Belize City, it offers a glimpse into the life and architecture of the ancient Maya civilization.

The Great Blue Hole

Caracol

Caracol is the largest Mayan archaeological site in Belize, located deep in the Chiquibul Forest. It offers visitors a chance to explore massive pyramids and learn about the Maya's incredible achievements.
