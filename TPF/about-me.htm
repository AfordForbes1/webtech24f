Hello There!

Hello and welcome! I’m Amber, a Belizean American with a deep connection to the beautiful country of Belize. Growing up in a Belizean household and visiting frequently, I’ve come to know and cherish the landscapes, cultures, and traditions that make Belize so special. As a senior at Babson College, concentrating in technology and entrepreneurship, I’m creating this website to share my passion for Belize with others and provide insights for anyone curious about this incredible destination.

My Journey

After spending years exploring the hidden gems of my home country, I decided to create this website as a way to share my love for Belize and its incredible offerings. I want to inspire others to visit this beautiful country and discover its breathtaking landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. This site is not just a travel guide; it's my personal project, where I combine my knowledge of technology and my entrepreneurial spirit to offer visitors useful tips, advice, and insights on how to make the most of their Belizean adventures. From popular landmarks to lesser-known spots, you'll find all the information you need here.
