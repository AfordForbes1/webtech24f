Project Proposal

OBJECTIVE

The objective of this website is to provide an engaging and informative platform for travelers interested in visiting Belize. The website will highlight popular landmarks, activities, and the unique beauty of Belizeâ€™s small islands. It will guide users in exploring various destinations within the country and help them plan their trips by offering insights into what to see and do. The site will be a resource, focusing on key locations and activities, ensuring a streamlined and enjoyable user experience.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website includes tourists, travel enthusiasts, and adventure seekers looking for information on Belize as a travel destination. This site will cater to individuals who are researching travel destinations, planning a vacation, or looking for inspiration for their next trip, with an emphasis on nature, culture, and adventure in Belize.

Page Contents

Each page will have a specific focus, as follows:

  • Home Page: An overview of Belize as a travel destination, with featured images and brief descriptions of key highlights.
  • About Belize: A brief history and cultural background of Belize, including its natural attractions and diverse ecosystems.
  • Popular Landmarks: A dedicated page for popular landmarks in Belize such as the Great Blue Hole, Xunantunich, and Caye Caulker.
  • Things to Do: A page highlighting activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, hiking, and exploring Mayan ruins.
  • Contact Us: Contact information and inquiries for travel advice.
  • Sitemap: A guide to the structure of the website.
  • Credits: Acknowledgements of any sources used for images or information.

    • Additional Pages

    • Islands of Belize: A gallery and description of the small islands, their attractions, and how to reach them.
    • Belize Travel Tips: Essential travel information like when to visit, visa requirements, and currency tips.
